# ⚡ WattWise

### AI-Powered Smart Energy Optimization System

## Overview

WattWise is an AI-driven platform designed to improve energy efficiency and reduce grid stress by analyzing electricity consumption patterns. The system uses Machine Learning and Agentic AI to predict energy demand, detect anomalies, and provide real-time insights for smarter energy management.

## Problem Statement

* Excess rooftop solar energy is wasted because households cannot trade power locally.
* Nearly 30% of residential solar energy remains unused during low-demand periods.
* EV charging demand is expected to triple by 2030, increasing peak-hour grid stress.
* Power systems often detect faults only after outages affect users.
* Smart meter data is generated in huge amounts, but utilities lack AI systems to convert it into actionable insights.

## Key Features

* AI-based energy demand prediction
* Agentic AI for intelligent decision making
* Real-time energy monitoring dashboard
* Smart anomaly and fault detection
* Energy optimization and grid load balancing
